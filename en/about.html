About MGA

A law firm in Lisbon grounded in rigour, responsibility and close support.

Miguel, Goes & Associados is a law firm founded in 1989, based in the heart of Lisbon and guided by rigour, ethics and closeness to its clients. We understand the practice of law as an exercise in responsibility, clarity and commitment to legally appropriate solutions for the interests of those who seek our advice.

Framework

The firm is defined by a vision of legal practice that combines experience, professional dedication and constant attention to the development of the legal and economic contexts in which its clients operate.

More than a strictly formal intervention, we favour a serious and careful approach, centred on understanding each situation, the quality of legal analysis and consistent support throughout each matter or professional relationship.

Principles of practice

Ethics

Responsible, independent conduct in accordance with the professional ethical duties of the profession.

Rigour

Careful analysis, technical preparation and attention to the legal framework of each matter.

Closeness

A professional relationship based on listening, clarity in communication and effective support.

Trust

Discretion, consistency and a sense of responsibility in representing the interests entrusted to us.

Approach

The firm views the practice of law as one of closeness and high standards, in which legal knowledge must be placed at the service of clear, well-founded and useful responses for each client.

We seek to ensure a coherent approach, attentive to detail and directed towards the building of serious and lasting professional relationships, based on trust and the responsible handling of each matter.
