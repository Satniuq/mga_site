About the Firm

A legal practice grounded in rigour, responsibility, professional discretion and close client support.

Miguel, Goes & Associados is a law firm founded in 1998, based in Lisbon, whose practice is grounded in technical rigour, a strong sense of responsibility, professional discretion and close support to its clients.

We understand the practice of law as an activity founded on trust, high standards and clarity, directed towards the serious analysis of each situation and the development of legally consistent responses suited to the specific reality of those who seek our advice.

Framework

A firm guided by continuity, technical judgement and attention to the concrete context of each matter.

The firm is defined by a legal practice that combines experience, continuity and attention to the effective context in which clients’ interests arise, whether in personal and asset-related matters or in business and contentious matters.

More than a merely formal intervention, we favour a careful approach that is technically sound and attentive to the way in which each legal issue should be framed, prepared and followed over time.

Principles of Practice

A way of working grounded in high standards, trust and professional responsibility.

Ethics

Responsible, independent conduct in accordance with the professional and ethical duties of the legal profession.

Rigour

Careful analysis, sound technical preparation and close attention to the legal framework of each matter.

Closeness

A professional relationship based on listening, clarity in communication and effective legal support.

Trust

Discretion, consistency and a strong sense of responsibility in representing the interests entrusted to us.

Our Approach

Clear, well-grounded legal support tailored to the reality of each client and each matter.

MG&A seeks to provide legal assistance that is clear, well-grounded and genuinely useful, with close attention to detail, the quality of analysis and the consistency of the support provided.

We value serious and lasting professional relationships, built on trust, on the willingness to understand each case properly and on the responsibility with which every matter is handled.
