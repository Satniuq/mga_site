About the Firm

A legal practice grounded in rigour, responsibility and close support.

Miguel, Goes & Associados is a law firm founded in 1989, based in Lisbon, whose practice is grounded in technical rigour, a sense of responsibility, professional discretion and close client support.

We understand the practice of law as an activity based on trust, high standards and clarity, directed towards the serious analysis of each situation and the development of legally sound responses suited to the specific reality of those who seek our advice.

Framework

The firm is defined by a legal practice that combines experience, continuity and attention to the effective context in which clients’ interests arise, whether in personal and asset-related matters or in business and contentious matters.

More than a merely formal intervention, we favour a careful approach that is technically sound and attentive to the way in which each legal issue should be framed, prepared and followed over time.

Principles of Practice

Ethics

Responsible, independent conduct in accordance with the professional duties of the legal profession.

Rigour

Careful analysis, technical preparation and attention to the legal framework of each matter.

Closeness

A professional relationship based on listening, clarity in communication and effective support.

Trust

Discretion, consistency and a sense of responsibility in representing the interests entrusted to us.

Approach

MGA seeks to ensure legal assistance that is clear, well grounded and useful, with attention to detail, the quality of analysis and the consistency of the support provided.

We value serious and lasting professional relationships, built on trust, availability to understand each case and the responsibility with which each matter is handled.
