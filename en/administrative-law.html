Administrative Law

Legal advice on dealings with public authorities, administrative procedures and administrative acts in Portugal.

We assist with administrative law matters involving proceedings before public authorities, licences, authorisations, notifications, administrative acts and positions adopted by the Administration.

This area is particularly relevant where an administrative decision affects the client’s activity, requires a formal response or depends on the correct legal framing of the applicable procedure.

What We Handle

Administrative procedures and dealings with public authorities
Licences, authorisations, approvals and other permissive acts
Notifications, orders, approvals and refusals
Preparation of applications, submissions and responses to the Administration
Analysis of administrative acts and their legal effects
Administrative offences and procedural response

Procedures and Relations with Public Authorities

We provide legal advice in administrative procedures that require the submission of applications, responses to notifications, compliance with formal requirements and follow-up with municipalities, public institutes, registries and other administrative bodies.

In many cases, the key issue lies in understanding which procedure applies, which elements must be submitted and how best to structure the client’s position before the Administration.

Licences, Authorisations and Administrative Acts

We assist in matters dependent on licences, authorisations, approvals, opinions and other administrative acts with personal, patrimonial or business impact.

We also advise on the analysis of administrative orders, refusals, constraints and other decisions, assessing their scope, underlying grounds and the room for action still available in each case.

Administrative Offences and Response to Administrative Action

Certain interventions by the Administration may have a sanctioning nature or give rise to reports, notifications, imposed duties and administrative offence decisions.

In these situations, the analysis of the procedure, the relevant deadlines and the reasoning adopted may be decisive in defining the legally appropriate response and protecting the position of the person concerned.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

Administrative law frequently intersects with investment, real estate and planning, public procurement and litigation and arbitration, depending on the nature of the matter and the stage of the specific situation.

If you require support with administrative procedures, licences, authorisations, administrative acts or dealings with public authorities, you may contact MG&A.
