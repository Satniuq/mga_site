Civil Law

Legal advice on civil law matters with contractual, patrimonial and personal relevance in Portugal.

We assist with civil law matters relating to contracts, property, leases, condominium issues, sale and purchase, gifts, civil liability and other patrimonial relationships between private parties.

This area is particularly relevant where it is necessary to clarify rights and obligations, prevent disputes or place a situation with personal or financial impact within the proper legal framework.

Contracts and Patrimonial Relationships

We provide legal advice on the analysis, preparation and review of contracts, as well as on matters involving breach, interpretation of clauses, the parties’ responsibilities and the legal consequences of acts or omissions.

Many civil law issues begin precisely here: understanding what was agreed, what binds each party and what legal solution exists in the event of breach or disagreement.

Property, Leases and Condominium Matters

We assist with matters relating to ownership and use of assets, leases, occupation of property, charges, condominium issues, use of common areas and other everyday civil law situations with patrimonial impact.

These matters often require a combined reading of the facts, the documentation and the applicable legal framework, particularly where there is a dispute between private parties or a need to clarify legal positions.

Sale and Purchase, Gifts and Civil Liability

We also provide legal support in transactions between private parties, such as sale and purchase and gifts, as well as in matters of civil liability and compensation for damages.

In these cases, the legal analysis should ensure clarity as to the validity of the act, its patrimonial effects and the responsibilities that may arise from the conduct of the parties involved.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

Civil law frequently intersects with investment and real estate, family law, succession and inheritance, notarial services and formal acts and litigation and arbitration, depending on the nature of the specific matter.

If you require support with contracts, property, leases, condominium matters, sale and purchase, gifts or civil liability, you may contact MG&A.
