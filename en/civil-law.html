Civil Law

Legal advice on civil law matters with contractual, asset-related and personal relevance in Portugal.

We assist with civil law matters relating to contracts, property, leases, condominium matters, sale and purchase, gifts, civil liability and other asset-related relationships between private parties.

This area is particularly relevant where it is necessary to clarify rights and obligations, prevent disputes or frame a situation with personal or financial impact within the proper legal context.

Leases and landlord-tenant relations

Contracts and Asset-Related Relationships

We provide legal advice on the analysis, drafting and review of contracts, as well as on matters involving breach, interpretation of clauses, the parties’ obligations and the legal consequences of acts or omissions.

Many civil law issues begin precisely here: understanding what was agreed, what binds each party and what legal solution may exist in the event of breach or disagreement.

Property, Leases and Condominium Matters

We assist with matters relating to ownership and use of assets, leases, occupation of property, charges, condominium issues, use of common areas and other everyday civil law situations with financial impact.

These matters often require a combined reading of the facts, the documentation and the applicable legal framework, particularly where there is a dispute between private parties or a need to clarify legal positions.

Sale and Purchase, Gifts and Civil Liability

We also provide legal support in transactions between private parties, such as sale and purchase and gifts, as well as in matters of civil liability and compensation for damages.

In these cases, the legal analysis should ensure clarity as to the validity of the act, its financial effects and the responsibilities that may arise from the conduct of the parties involved.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

Civil law frequently intersects with investment, real estate and urban planning, family law, family, succession and estate planning, notarial services and formal acts and litigation and arbitration, depending on the nature of the specific matter.

If you require legal support on contracts, property, leases, condominium matters, sale and purchase, gifts or civil liability, you may contact MG&A.
