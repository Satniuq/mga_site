MG&A

Corporate and Commercial Law

Legal advice for companies, shareholders, entrepreneurs and investors on corporate structure, commercial contracts and the legal organisation of business activity in Portugal.

We assist with company incorporation and reorganisation, shareholder relations, share transfers, corporate governance and commercial contracts relevant to the company’s activity.

This area is particularly important where the entry or exit of shareholders is at stake, where internal rules need to be formalised, where investment must be protected or where a growing business requires a sound legal structure.

What We Handle

Company incorporation and definition of the corporate structure
Share transfers and entry or exit of shareholders
Articles of association, shareholders’ agreements, minutes and resolutions
Corporate reorganisation and changes to the company structure
Commercial contracts with clients, suppliers, partners and investors
Prevention of shareholder disputes and protection of shareholders’ positions

Companies, Shareholders and Governance

We provide legal advice on matters concerning the internal operation of the company, the position of shareholders and the definition of the rules governing decision-making, management and the transfer of corporate interests.

The aim is to provide legal stability to the business and reduce future vulnerabilities, particularly at stages of growth, investment or changes in the shareholder structure.

Commercial Contracts and Business Activity

We assist with the preparation, review and negotiation of commercial contracts that are relevant to the operation of the business, including relationships with clients, suppliers, distributors, partners and investors.

This contractual component should be aligned with the company’s corporate structure and with the specific objectives of the transaction or business operation.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

This area frequently intersects with investment, real estate and urban planning, employment and labour law and litigation and arbitration, particularly in business, asset-related or shareholder dispute contexts.

If you require legal support on company incorporation, share transfers, commercial contracts or corporate reorganisation, you may contact MG&A.
