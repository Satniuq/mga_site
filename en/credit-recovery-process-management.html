Credit Recovery and Process Management

Legal support for companies and institutional clients in debt collection, process handling and the organisation of matters involving volume, repetition and the need for ongoing response.

We assist clients who require an organised legal response in matters involving credit recovery, management of notifications, deadline control and the ongoing handling of legal matters.

This area is particularly relevant where the client is not dealing with a single isolated dispute, but rather with a set of matters requiring structured intake, triage, follow-up, consistency of action and the capacity to respond on a continuing basis.

What We Handle

Judicial and out-of-court credit recovery
Management of high-volume matters requiring ongoing handling
Review of and response to judicial notifications
Deadline control and procedural follow-up
Negotiation and formalisation of payment arrangements
Monitoring compliance with agreed payment terms
Response to situations of default
Organisation of procedural information and reporting to the client

Credit Recovery and Response to Default

We provide legal advice on debt collection and credit recovery, both at the out-of-court stage and at the judicial stage, depending on the nature of the matter and the viability of a solution.

In many situations, the central issue is not merely whether proceedings should be initiated, but rather how quickly to decide whether a settlement should be attempted, when judicial action should be pursued and how the matter should be monitored after the initial response.

Notifications, Deadlines and Process Organisation

We assist with the receipt, review and internal routing of notifications, as well as with deadline control and the definition of the legally appropriate course of action in each situation.

This support is particularly important where there is procedural volume, a need for a uniform response and an ongoing requirement to organise the information relevant to each matter.

Ongoing Handling and Overall Visibility

The management of high-volume matters requires more than isolated intervention. It requires follow-up, working method, internal control and the ability to give the client a clear view of the status of ongoing matters.

For that reason, legal support in this area seeks to ensure consistency of action, timely response and practical coordination between the legal work, the procedural information and the client's operational needs.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

This area frequently intersects with litigation and arbitration, corporate and commercial law and employment and labour law, depending on the origin of the credit, the nature of the underlying legal relationship and the client's business framework.

If you require legal support in credit recovery, notifications management, deadline control or the ongoing handling of legal matters, you may contact MG&A.
