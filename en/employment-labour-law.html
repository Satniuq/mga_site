Employment and Labour Law

Legal advice on employment relations, the organisation of the employment relationship and the management of conflict between employers and employees.

We assist with employment and labour law matters relating to recruitment, employment contracts, the performance of the employment relationship, the parties’ duties, disciplinary procedures, termination of employment and labour disputes.

This area is particularly relevant where it is necessary to structure the employment relationship correctly, prevent legal risks or respond to situations of breach, conflict or breakdown of the employment relationship.

What We Handle

Employment contracts and the legal framework of the employment relationship
Recruitment and organisation of labour relations
Labour duties, changes affecting the employment relationship and breach of obligations
Disciplinary procedures and sanctions
Dismissal, termination by agreement and termination of employment
Labour disputes and contentious support

Recruitment and Organisation of Labour Relations

We provide legal advice on the preparation and analysis of employment contracts and on the legal framework of the employment relationship from its outset, with attention to the role performed, the agreed conditions and the specific structure of the work arrangement.

A clear definition of the employment relationship from the initial stage is often decisive in reducing future conflict and ensuring greater legal stability for both parties.

Performance of Employment, Duties and Changes Affecting the Relationship

We assist with matters arising during the performance of the employment contract, including the duties of the employee and employer, changes affecting the relationship, breach of obligations and other situations of disciplinary or organisational relevance.

These matters often require a concrete analysis of the facts, the available documentation and the context in which the employment relationship develops.

Disciplinary Procedures and Termination of Employment

We also provide legal support in disciplinary procedures, dismissal, termination by agreement, expiry, resignation and other forms of termination of the employment relationship.

At this stage, it is essential to ensure the correct legal framework of the situation, compliance with the applicable formalities and proper protection of the client’s position.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

Employment and labour law frequently intersects with corporate and commercial law, litigation and arbitration and, in certain contexts, with criminal law, particularly where conflict, liability or sanctioning issues arise.

If you require support with employment contracts, disciplinary procedures, dismissal, termination of employment or labour disputes, you may contact MG&A.
