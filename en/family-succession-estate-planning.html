Succession and Inheritance

Legal advice on succession matters, transfer of assets and the organisation of succession in Portugal.

We assist with matters relating to inheritance, wills, estate division, identification of heirs, acceptance or renunciation of inheritance, as well as other issues connected with the transfer of assets upon death.

This area is particularly relevant where it is necessary to organise succession in advance, clarify the position of heirs or deal with the composition and division of the estate.

What We Handle

Wills and the legal framing of succession wishes
Identification of heirs
Acceptance or renunciation of inheritance
Estate division and determination of inheritance shares
Transfer of assets and patrimonial regularisation
Succession planning and prevention of future disputes

Wills and Succession Planning

We provide legal advice on the preparation and structuring of succession planning solutions, including wills and other legal arrangements for the transfer of assets.

In many cases, a well-structured succession plan helps reduce uncertainty, prevent disputes among heirs and ensure greater clarity as to the destination of assets and the wishes of the person whose estate is concerned.

Heirs, Inheritance and Estate Division

We assist with determining the position of heirs, the composition of the estate and the issues that arise at the stage of estate division, including identification of assets and definition of the rights of each interested party.

These situations often require careful analysis of the family structure, the available documentation and the legal framework applicable to the particular succession.

Acceptance, Renunciation and Patrimonial Regularisation

We also provide legal support in matters concerning acceptance or renunciation of inheritance, as well as subsequent patrimonial regularisation whenever it is necessary to formalise the transfer of assets or stabilise the legal status of the estate.

Clarity at this stage is important in order to avoid uncertainty, future disputes and difficulties in the administration or disposal of the transferred assets.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

This area frequently intersects with family law, civil law, investment and real estate and notarial services and formal acts, particularly where succession involves real estate, complex family structures or documentary formalisation of succession acts.

If you require support with wills, inheritance, estate division, identification of heirs or succession planning, you may contact MG&A.
