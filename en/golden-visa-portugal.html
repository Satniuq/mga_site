Golden Visa in Portugal

Legal advice on the Golden Visa in Portugal, including eligible investment, online submission of the application and support through biometrics and residence permit issuance.

We assist with matters relating to the Golden Visa in Portugal, generally aimed at third-country nationals who intend to obtain residence through an eligible investment route under the applicable legal framework.

In many cases, the process does not depend on an initial application through a consular post, but rather on the preparation of the investment, collection of documents, online submission of the application and subsequent travel to Portugal for biometrics.

Eligible Investment and Structure of the Operation

The central issue in the Golden Visa is the actual eligibility of the investment. The analysis must take into account the route chosen, the origin and availability of funds, the way the operation is structured and the documentation required to demonstrate that the legal requirements are effectively met.

Depending on the applicable legal framework, relevant routes may include eligible funds, incorporation or reinforcement of a commercial company meeting the legal requirements, job creation, support for scientific research and support for artistic production or preservation of cultural heritage.

Documentation, Online Submission and Biometrics

The strength of the process often depends on the quality of the documentary preparation. Key elements include identity documents, criminal record certificates, banking documentation, proof of the origin and transfer of funds, evidence of execution of the investment and documents issued by the Portuguese entities involved in the operation.

In many cases, the application is submitted online through the relevant portal after the process has been organised in advance. Physical presence in Portugal tends to be concentrated mainly at the biometrics stage, which is one of the most relevant practical features of this route.

Maintenance of the Investment, Minimum Stay and Continuity

The Golden Visa should not be viewed only at the moment of the initial application. From the outset, it is important to safeguard the maintenance of the investment, documentary consistency over time, renewal of the residence permit and the overall stability of the solution adopted.

This route is also often sought because it may allow a lower physical presence in Portugal than other residence regimes, which makes it essential to structure the process prudently from the beginning and with attention to later stages of renewal and continuity of status.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

The Golden Visa in Portugal frequently intersects with residence in Portugal, corporate and commercial law, investment, real estate and planning, succession and inheritance and family reunification, depending on the structure of the investment and the client’s objectives in Portugal.
