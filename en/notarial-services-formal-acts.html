Notarial Services and Formal Acts

Legal assistance with documentary formalities and formal legal acts in Portugal, with technical rigour and attention to the requirements of each act.

We assist with notarial services and formal acts required for personal, family, asset-related and business matters, including signature certifications, authentications, certified copies, powers of attorney and other documentary formalities.

This area is particularly relevant where the validity, enforceability or practical use of a document depends on compliance with specific legal formalities, whether for use in Portugal or abroad.

Certifications, Authentications and Certified Copies

We provide legal assistance with documentary acts intended to confer formal reliability, evidential force or legal effectiveness on documents, including signature certifications, authentications and certified copies.

These formalities are often required in connection with contracts, declarations, authorisations, foreign documentation and documents that must be presented before public authorities, private entities or institutions in Portugal and abroad.

Powers of Attorney, Authenticated Private Documents and Other Formal Acts

We assist with the preparation and formalisation of powers of attorney, authenticated private documents and other acts whose legal effectiveness depends on the correct documentary form and verification of the parties, their powers and the content of the act.

In these matters, it is essential to ensure that the wording of the document, the powers granted, the identification of the intervening parties and the supporting documentation are aligned with the purpose for which the act is intended.

Certified Translations, Marital Consent and Authorisations for Minors

We also assist with documentary situations that frequently arise in international mobility, family, succession and asset-related matters, including certified translations, marital consent and authorisations for minors to travel.

These acts often require careful coordination between identification documents, family documents, foreign documents and the legal requirements of the entity before which the document will be used.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

This area frequently intersects with residence in Portugal, investment, real estate and urban planning, family law, family, succession and estate planning, civil law and corporate and commercial law, depending on the type of document or formal act involved.

If you require legal assistance with signature certifications, authentications, certified copies, powers of attorney, certified translations, marital consent or authorisations for minors, you may contact MG&A.
