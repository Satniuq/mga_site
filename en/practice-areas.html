Practice Areas

Legal advice for individuals, families and companies in Portugal

We provide legal advice through an integrated approach, articulating different matters whenever required by the client’s circumstances, namely in the context of residence, investment, corporate structuring, real estate, succession and the protection of interests.

Residence, International Mobility and Relocation to Portugal

We advise individuals and families seeking to live, invest or organise their interests in Portugal, providing legal support in matters relating to residence, investment, assets, asset organisation and real estate.

Commercial and Corporate Law

Business activity in Portugal frequently raises issues requiring proper legal framing and coordination in corporate and contractual matters. We provide such support in the context of company incorporation and reorganisation, shareholder relations, corporate governance and commercial agreements.

Investment, Real Estate and Urban Planning

We provide legal advice in investment and real estate matters in Portugal, with support tailored to acquisition, lease, licensing, urban planning framework and asset organisation contexts.

Employment and Labour Law

We provide legal advice on employment matters, assisting companies, employers and employees in recruitment, hiring and contract preparation, the follow-up of the employment relationship and the termination of employment.

Civil Law

We provide legal advice on Civil Law matters relevant to the organisation and protection of personal and asset-related interests in Portugal, including contractual relationships, property, lease, condominium matters, sale and purchase, gifts and other civil-law situations.

Family Law

We provide legal advice on family matters, assisting in situations relating to marriage, divorce, separation, parental responsibilities, maintenance and other family relationships, with support adapted to the personal and asset-related nature of each case.

Succession and Inheritance

We provide legal advice on succession matters, assisting in the organisation and transfer of assets in the context of inheritance, wills, division of estates and succession planning, with particular attention to the articulation with family, asset and real estate issues.

Administrative Law

We provide legal advice on Administrative Law matters, assisting in relations with public entities, administrative procedures, licences, authorisations and other administrative acts relevant to individuals, investors and companies in Portugal.

Public Procurement

We provide legal advice on public procurement matters, assisting companies and other interested parties in the preparation, participation and follow-up of pre-contractual procedures, as well as in the performance of public contracts.

Criminal Law

We provide legal advice and representation in criminal matters, assisting individuals and companies in situations of investigation, accusation and defence, as well as in contexts requiring immediate response, discretion and rigorous technical framing.

Notarial Services and Formal Acts

We assist with notarial acts and document formalisation, including signature certification, authentications, certifications, powers of attorney and other instruments required for the formal validity of legal acts in Portugal.

Litigation and Arbitration

We provide legal representation in disputes before judicial and arbitral courts, assisting in the defence of personal, asset-related and business interests in Portugal, with an approach adapted to the nature and complexity of each situation.

We provide legal support tailored to the nature of each situation, articulating different practice areas whenever required by the client’s circumstances, with technical rigour, discretion and attention to the personal, asset-related or business context of each case.
