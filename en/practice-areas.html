Practice Areas

Legal advice for individuals, families and companies in Portugal

We provide legal advice on an integrated basis, coordinating different matters whenever required by the client's circumstances, namely in the context of residence, investment, corporate structuring, real estate, succession and the defence of interests.

Residence, International Mobility and Establishment in Portugal

We assist individuals and families who wish to live, invest or organise their interests in Portugal, providing legal advice on matters of residence, investment, assets, asset organisation and real estate.

Commercial and Corporate Law

Business activity in Portugal frequently raises issues that require legal framework and coordination in corporate and contractual matters. We provide that assistance in the context of the incorporation and reorganisation of companies, relations between shareholders, corporate governance and commercial contracts.

Investment, Real Estate and Urban Planning

We provide legal advice on investment and real estate matters in Portugal, with assistance tailored to contexts of acquisition, lease, licensing, urban planning framework and asset organisation.

Employment Law

We provide legal advice on employment matters, assisting companies, employers and employees in recruitment, hiring and the preparation of contracts, monitoring the employment relationship and termination of the employment bond.

Civil Law

We provide legal advice on Civil Law matters relevant to the organisation and protection of personal and asset-related interests in Portugal, including contractual relations, property, lease, condominium, purchase and sale, gifts and other legal situations arising in civil life.

Family

We provide legal advice on family matters, assisting in situations connected with marriage, divorce, separation, regulation of parental responsibilities, maintenance and other family relationships, with action tailored to the personal and asset-related nature of each case.

Succession and Inheritance

We provide legal advice on succession matters, assisting in the organisation and transfer of assets in the context of inheritance, wills, partition and succession planning, with particular attention to coordination with family, asset-related and real estate issues.

Administrative Law

We provide legal advice on Administrative Law matters, assisting in relations with public entities, administrative procedures, licences, authorisations and other administrative acts relevant to individuals, investors and companies in Portugal.

Public Procurement

We provide legal advice on public procurement matters, assisting companies and other interested parties in the preparation, participation and monitoring of pre-contractual procedures, as well as in the performance of public contracts.

Criminal Law

We provide legal advice and representation in criminal matters, assisting individuals and companies in situations of investigation, prosecution and defence, as well as in contexts that require an immediate response, discretion and rigorous technical framework.

Solicitor Services and Notarial Acts

We provide assistance in notarial acts and document formalisation, including signature certifications, authentications, certifications, powers of attorney and other instruments necessary for the formal validity of legal acts in Portugal.

Litigation and Arbitration

We provide legal representation and advocacy in disputes brought before judicial and arbitral courts, assisting in the defence of personal, asset-related and business interests in Portugal, with action tailored to the nature and complexity of each situation.

We provide legal assistance tailored to the nature of each situation, coordinating different practice areas whenever required by the client's circumstances, with technical rigour, discretion and attention to the personal, asset-related or business context of each case.
