Legal Information

General information on the use of this website and the applicable framework.

The protection of personal data is treated seriously and in accordance with criteria of necessity, appropriateness and respect for the rights of data subjects.

Data Collected

Only the data strictly necessary for contact purposes, responding to requests or compliance with legal obligations is collected.

Purposes of Processing

The data is processed for the purposes of responding, scheduling meetings or providing legal services, on the basis of the data subject's consent or compliance with applicable legal obligations.

Rights of Data Subjects

The data subject may exercise their rights of access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability and objection, and for that purpose should use the contact details indicated on the contacts page.

Data Retention

The data is retained only for the period necessary for the purpose that motivated its collection or while the contractual relationship or legal obligation justifying that retention remains in force.

Security

Appropriate technical and organisational measures are adopted to protect personal data against loss, improper access or unauthorised disclosure.

For any question related to this matter, you may use the means made available on the contacts page.
