Privacy Policy

Information on the collection and processing of personal data in connection with the use of this website.

This Privacy Policy describes, in general terms, how Miguel, Goes & Associados – Sociedade de Advogados, SP, RL processes personal data in the context of the use of this website and the contacts made through the means provided herein.

Personal data is processed in accordance with Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (General Data Protection Regulation) and the applicable national legislation.

Data controller

The controller of the personal data collected through this website is Miguel, Goes & Associados – Sociedade de Advogados, SP, RL.

For privacy-related matters or for the exercise of rights, the user may use the contact details made available on the Contacts page.

Personal data that may be collected

Depending on how the user interacts with the website, data such as name, email address, telephone number and any other information voluntarily provided through forms, messages or contact requests may be processed.

Technical browsing data strictly necessary for the operation, security and administration of the website may also be collected, depending on the technical configuration adopted.

Purposes and legal bases for processing

Personal data may be processed for purposes such as responding to contact requests, managing communications, providing requested information, complying with legal obligations and protecting legitimate interests relating to the security and management of the website.

Depending on the case, processing is based on pre-contractual steps taken at the request of the data subject, compliance with legal obligations, the data subject’s consent where applicable, or the legitimate interests of the controller, provided that these are compatible with the rights and freedoms of the data subject.

Data retention

Personal data is retained only for the period necessary for the purposes that justify its processing, without prejudice to any retention periods resulting from law, regulatory duties or the need to defend rights in any administrative, arbitral or judicial proceedings.

Disclosure to third parties

Personal data is not disclosed to third parties, except where this is necessary to comply with legal obligations, for the technical management of the website, for the provision of support services by entities acting on behalf of MG&A, or where such disclosure is legitimately required by the nature of the request submitted by the data subject.

Where applicable, such entities are subject to duties of confidentiality and to appropriate data protection obligations.

Rights of data subjects

Under the applicable legislation, the data subject may request access to their personal data, its rectification, erasure, restriction of processing or portability, and may also object to processing where the respective legal conditions are met.

Where processing is based on consent, the data subject may withdraw it at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing carried out up to that date.

Exercise of rights and complaints

Rights may be exercised through the contact details made available on this website. The data subject also has the right to lodge a complaint with the competent supervisory authority, without prejudice to any other applicable administrative or judicial remedy.

Cookies and browsing data

The website may use cookies or similar technologies, in particular to ensure its technical operation, improve the browsing experience and collect statistical information, depending on the configuration adopted.

Where applicable, the use of cookies that are not strictly necessary will depend on the consent options made available to the user.

Changes to this policy

MG&A may update this Privacy Policy whenever necessary, in particular as a result of legal, regulatory, technical or organisational changes. The version published on the website at any given time shall be deemed to be the version in force.

For any further clarification regarding the processing of personal data in connection with this website, you may use the contact details made available by MG&A.
