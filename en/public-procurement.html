Public Procurement

Legal advice on public tenders, pre-contractual procedures and the performance of public contracts in Portugal.

We assist economic operators and other interested parties in matters relating to public procurement, including participation in pre-contractual procedures, analysis of procurement documents, preparation of applications and tenders, review of decisions and legal issues arising during contract performance.

This area is particularly relevant where access to a public tender depends on strict procedural compliance, where the procurement documents raise legal concerns or where it is necessary to protect the client’s position before, during or after the award decision.

What We Handle

Public tenders and other pre-contractual procedures
Review of procurement documents and procedural rules
Applications, tenders and qualification requirements
Requests for clarification and procedural communications
Challenges to exclusion and award decisions
Legal issues in the performance of public contracts

Procurement Documents and Participation in the Procedure

We provide legal advice on the analysis of procurement documents, the legal framework of the procedure and the conditions for participation by the interested economic operator.

In many cases, the decisive issue lies in understanding the procedural requirements correctly, identifying legal risks in advance and ensuring that the application or tender is prepared in a manner that is complete, coherent and compliant.

Exclusion, Award Decisions and Protection of Position

We assist in matters involving exclusion decisions, qualification issues, award decisions and other procedural acts capable of affecting the client’s legal position.

These situations require careful attention to deadlines, the grounds relied upon by the contracting authority, the content of the documents submitted and the most appropriate legal response for the defence of the client’s rights and interests.

Public Contracts and Performance Issues

Public procurement matters do not end with the award decision. Legal issues may also arise during the performance of the public contract, including interpretation of obligations, contractual changes, compliance matters and disputes connected with execution.

For that reason, legal support in this area should address both the pre-contractual stage and the contractual stage, with a view to ensuring legal certainty throughout the life of the public procurement matter.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

Public procurement frequently intersects with administrative law, corporate and commercial law and litigation and arbitration, depending on the nature of the procedure, the contracting authority and the contractual issues involved.

If you require legal support on public tenders, procurement documents, exclusion or award decisions, or legal issues in the performance of public contracts, you may contact MGA.
