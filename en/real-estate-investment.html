We assist clients with property acquisition and sale, leases, due diligence, asset organisation, property regularisation and urban planning matters connected with real estate investment in Portugal.

Property Transactions and Due Diligence

We provide legal advice on the acquisition and sale of property in Portugal, with particular attention to the legal status of the asset, the consistency of its documentation, the review of registry and tax elements and the identification of risks that may affect the transaction.

Proper due diligence is often decisive not only for the completion of the deal, but also for preventing future disputes, limitations on use or unexpected burdens affecting the property.

Urban Planning, Licensing and Property Regularisation

Real estate matters frequently require analysis that goes beyond the transaction itself. It may be necessary to assess licensing history, planning constraints, administrative acts, construction legality, changes to use, compliance with municipal rules and the feasibility of the intended project or occupation.

In these cases, legal support should combine documentary review, contact with the relevant authorities and a precise understanding of the urban planning and administrative framework applicable to the asset.

Investment, Assets and Legal Structure

Real estate investment is often connected with broader legal decisions concerning residence, company structure, asset protection, succession planning or the organisation of family wealth.

For that reason, legal advice in this area should not treat the property in isolation, but instead consider how the acquisition, holding, use or transfer of the asset fits within the client’s wider personal, family or business structure.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

This area frequently intersects with residence in Portugal, the Golden Visa, corporate and commercial law, administrative law, family, succession and estate planning and notarial services and formal acts, depending on the nature of the property, the investment model and the client’s broader objectives in Portugal.

If you require legal support on property acquisition or sale, leases, real estate due diligence, licensing, urban planning or asset structuring in Portugal, you may contact MG&A.
