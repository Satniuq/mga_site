Residence and International Mobility in Portugal

Legal advice for individuals and families seeking to live, invest or organise their interests in Portugal.

We advise clients who intend to relocate to Portugal or to structure their presence in the country on a legally sound basis. Our work is developed through an integrated approach, bringing together matters of residence, international mobility, investment, assets, real estate and the organisation of personal and business interests.

In many cases, a client’s situation does not concern a single isolated issue. Residence in Portugal may require coordination with the acquisition or lease of property, asset organisation, company structuring, family matters and document preparation. It is precisely in that articulation that we provide rigorous and continuous legal support.

Legal Framework for Residence and Relocation to Portugal

We provide legal advice on matters related to residence in Portugal and international mobility, assisting clients who seek to settle in the country, reorganise their personal and asset-related affairs or prepare their relocation to Portugal on a legally consistent basis.

Our work includes the analysis of each specific situation, the legal and documentary framework involved, the preparation of supporting elements and the coordination of the different legal matters that may arise in connection with relocation to Portugal.

Legal Support Provided

Our support may include, in particular, the analysis of the applicable legal framework, the organisation of relevant documentation, the coordination of asset or real estate operations, assistance with the structuring of family and business interests and the follow-up of the steps required in each case.

Whenever necessary, we articulate this area with other areas of the firm’s practice, namely investment, real estate, commercial and corporate law, family and succession, thereby ensuring a coherent and integrated view of the client’s position in Portugal.

Matters Commonly Handled

The matters most frequently associated with this area include:

the relocation of individuals and families to Portugal; the legal organisation of asset-related interests; coordination with investment and the acquisition or use of property; document preparation connected with a client’s presence in Portuguese territory; the legal structuring of corporate or business arrangements linked to the client’s activity; and coordination with family or succession matters involving an international element.

An Integrated and Careful Approach

Residence and international mobility often require an assessment that goes beyond a merely administrative dimension. In many cases, decisions with asset, real estate, corporate or family implications are at stake, and the appropriate legal framework must therefore be approached in a careful, coordinated and case-specific manner.

Our practice is built on that logic: understanding the client’s overall circumstances, identifying the relevant legal issues and ensuring technically solid support, without artificial simplifications or fragmented approaches.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

This area frequently intersects with matters relating to investment, real estate, asset organisation, commercial and corporate law, family and succession, as well as the protection of interests whenever complementary legal support becomes necessary.
