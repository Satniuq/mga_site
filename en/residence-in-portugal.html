Residence in Portugal

Legal advice on residence visas, family reunification and relocation to Portugal for individuals, families, investors and entrepreneurs.

We advise clients who intend to live, invest, work or organise their relocation to Portugal, including matters related to residence visas, international mobility, document structuring and preparation for the move.

This area is particularly relevant where residence in Portugal is connected with property rental or acquisition, company incorporation, asset organisation, family reunification and the practical legal framework of establishing a presence in the country.

Residence Visas and the Legal Framework of Relocation

We provide legal advice on residence matters in Portugal, including the assessment of the most suitable legal framework for each case, document preparation and coordination of the relevant steps required for relocation.

We assist in particular with matters relating to the D2 Visa, the D7 Visa, the Golden Visa and family reunification, depending on the client’s profile, source of income, nature of the investment and family structure.

Documentation, Preparation and Relocation to Portugal

Many residence matters require more than the main application itself. It is often necessary to deal with tax identification number matters, tax representation, bank account opening, foreign documentation, legalisations, translations, proof of accommodation and other supporting elements that contribute to the consistency of the process.

The correct preparation of this documentary base is often decisive in avoiding inconsistencies, delays or later difficulties, particularly where coordination is required between Portugal and the client’s country of origin.

Residence, Assets, Family and Business Activity

Residence in Portugal often intersects with asset, family and business decisions, such as property rental or acquisition, family organisation, investment, commencement of activity or company incorporation.

For that reason, legal support in this area should address not only the visa or residence title itself, but also the way in which the client’s presence in Portugal will be structured on a stable and legally coherent basis.

Connection with Other Practice Areas

This area frequently intersects with investment, real estate and urban planning, corporate and commercial law, family law, family, succession and estate planning and notarial services and formal acts, depending on how the client’s relocation to Portugal is organised.

If you require legal support on residence in Portugal, residence visas, the D2 Visa, the D7 Visa, the Golden Visa, family reunification or the preparation of your relocation to Portugal, you may contact MGA.
